BIOGRAPHY


Jankovsky is an Music Producer, Songwriter, Mixing Engineer and former Drummer for Ebony Bones. Raised in Estonia, Jankovsky found his passion for music after being gifted vinyl records by his uncle. He went on to learn the drums, and later earned degrees from Abbey Road Institute in London (Advanced Music Production & Sound Engineering) and University of Tallinn (Orchestral Conducting).

Jankovsky specializes in sound design, production, arrangement, recording and mixing. His music is most appropriately described as electronic pop or electro-pop, with some acoustic elements. He has worked with big industry names like Ebony Bones, Martin “Youth” Glover (producer of “Bitter Sweet Symphony” by The Verge), The Cult, Bananarama, Dido, and more.
