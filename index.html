INTRODUCTION


JANKOVSKY (ALSO KNOWN AS IGGY JAI) IS AN ESTONIAN MUSIC PRODUCER, SONGWRITER, MIXING ENGINEER, FORMER DRUMMER FOR EBONY BONES. HE STUDIED AND WORKED IN LONDON FROM 2010-2020. HE'S A GRADUATE OF ABBEY ROAD INSTITUTE, LONDON (ADVANCED MUSIC PRODUCTION AND SOUND ENGINEERING) AND UNIVERSITY OF TALLINN BA (ORCHESTRAL CONDUCTING).

JANKOVSKY SPECIALISES IN SOUND DESIGN, PRODUCTION, ARRANGEMENT, RECORDING AND MIXING.
